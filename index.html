Caffeinated Creations

Welcome to Caffeinated Creations! Our Coffee Shop is a creative strip of paradise where anyone is welcome to order some food and drink and just. Relax. Rain or shine our doors are always open!

At Caffeine Creations we do many unique drinks inspired by artists, famous Authors and famous inventors! We love setting ourselves apart from the crowd and standing out! We believe that everyone has a huge amount of creativity living inside of them.

We do Latte art workshops every Friday! From 1pm to 3pm! So if you are interested dont be afraid to join us! For all the Karaoke lovers out there, we host a live-song night every Tuesday evening from 4pm to 7pm!!
